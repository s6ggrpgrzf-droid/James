Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

DropPilot is an independent tool built for gig delivery and rideshare drivers. This page explains what data we collect and how it's used. Plain English, no surprises.

What we collect

  • No account, no sign-up. Using the app does not require an email address, name, or phone number.
  • Local-only preferences. Your favorites, language choice, intro-seen flag, and Elite-access token are stored in your browser's localStorage on your own device. We do not see them.
  • Stripe checkout. If you purchase Elite access, payment is processed by Stripe. We receive a one-time confirmation that the session was paid; we do not see or store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to the payment itself.
  • Support emails. If you email support@droppilot.app, we keep that conversation only for as long as needed to help you and process any refund.

What we do not collect

  • No tracking pixels, no third-party advertising cookies, no behavioral profiling.
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.
  • No data about your delivery routes, customers, ratings, or earnings.

Cookies & local storage

The site uses localStorage to remember your language, your Elite token, and which intro screens you've seen. Clearing your browser data removes everything.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties. The only third party involved is Stripe, and only for processing your payment.

Your rights

Because we don't hold an account or profile on you, there is generally nothing to delete on our end. If you've emailed support and want your message removed from our inbox, write to support@droppilot.app and we'll delete it.

Children

DropPilot is intended for adult gig workers and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle data, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy: support@droppilot.app.
