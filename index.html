The scripts, habits, and appeal letters here aren't from a business book. They come from years of customer-facing work — on both sides of the counter. 📞
Customer Service Operations
Managed customer service teams handling corporate client accounts — calls, complaints, escalations. Spent enough time in that world to understand how support agents are trained to read tickets, and what makes a complaint get resolved vs. get closed. That's what the appeal letters are built around.

Team management in high-volume retail — the checklists and shift tools in the app are built from that kind of work.
