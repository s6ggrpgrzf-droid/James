🚗 Deploy DropPilot

Publishes DropPilot to a free live URL entirely from your iPhone. Takes about 2 minutes.

1

Go to github.com/signup and create a free account. If you already have one, skip this.

2

Tap this link to create a token. Scroll all the way to the bottom and tap Generate token. Copy the long code it shows you — it starts with ghp_

3

Enter your GitHub username and paste the token below, then tap Deploy.

✓ DropPilot is live!
Tap the link below to open it. It may take 1-2 minutes to activate the first time — if it shows a 404, wait a minute and refresh.
To install on your home screen: open the link in Safari, tap the Share button, then tap Add to Home Screen.

Your token is only used once to upload the file. It is never stored or sent anywhere else.
